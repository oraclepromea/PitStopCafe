Our Story

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Copacabana, Pit Stop Cafe is more than just a coffee shop – it's a sanctuary where artisanal baking meets culinary passion. Since our opening, we've been dedicated to crafting exceptional empanadas, fresh-baked bread, and irresistible pies and desserts, all made with love and precision.

Our skilled bakers work daily to create authentic Bolivian empanadas with traditional and innovative fillings, while our fresh-baked bread serves as the foundation for our delicious sandwiches. From decadent desserts to savory pies, every item is handcrafted using the finest local ingredients. Whether you're craving a hearty empanada, a slice of homemade pie, or a perfectly brewed coffee to complement your treat, Pit Stop Cafe is your perfect destination.
